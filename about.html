About

Hey! I am Utsharga Anthony Cyril Rozario, a student currently enrolled at McMaster University and studying Software Engineering.

“Feed your curiosity, and it’ll lead you to the life you dream.”

The dream of being a programmer sparked within me at the age of 3 when I became fascinated with animation. My curiosity on how characters moved behind the scenes of the animated cartoons I watched, bloomed my dream to work as an engineer. My fascination soon evolved into a passion. I started programming around grade 8, with Visual Basic being my core as I wanted to develop games in Unity. While learning to code, I fell in love with it and became determined to be a software developer. I’ve ever since been learning about algorithms and coding.

But coding is not the only thing that intrigued me, the Discovery Channel introduced me to Space. Governed by Einstein on one side and Charles Babbage on the other, I got involved in Quantum Physics and eventually Quantum Computing.

I love programming. I like the industry. I like the people I meet with the same passion and ambition as me. I like to talk nerdy and embrace the geekiness in everyone. I hope to make this world a better place for everyone and if someday we cross paths, let’s make it a better place together.
